Chicken Curry


★★★★★ 150 Ratings

Description:

Sri Lankan Chicken Curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with chicken pieces, coconut milk, and a variety of spices. This curry is perfect for those who love bold and spicy flavors. The chicken is cooked in a rich coconut milk sauce infused with curry powder, turmeric, cinnamon, and cardamom, resulting in a hearty and satisfying dish. It is traditionally served with rice or flatbreads, making it a perfect meal for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lime juice (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Clean the chicken pieces thoroughly and set them aside.

Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add 1 chopped onion, 2 minced garlic cloves, and 1-inch minced ginger. Sauté until the onions are soft and golden.
  3. Add the 2 sliced green chilies and 1 sprig of curry leaves. Sauté for another minute.

Step 3: Add the Spices

  1. Add 1 tbsp curry powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp cinnamon powder, and 1/2 tsp cardamom powder. Stir well to coat the onions and garlic.

Step 4: Cook the Chicken

  1. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and mix well with the spices.
  2. Pour in 1/2 cup water and simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the chicken is tender.

Step 5: Add Coconut Milk

  1. Stir in 1 cup thick coconut milk and simmer for another 5-7 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 6: Adjust Seasoning and Serve

  1. Taste the curry and adjust the salt as needed.
  2. Add 1 tbsp lime juice (optional) for a fresh, tangy flavor.
  3. Serve hot with rice or flatbreads.

Tips for Perfect Chicken Curry:

  • Use fresh chicken for the best flavor.
  • Adjust the amount of spices to your taste preferences.
  • For a creamier curry, add more coconut milk.
  • Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to 2 days in the fridge.

Cultural Significance:

Sri Lankan Chicken Curry is a beloved dish that showcases the island's love for aromatic and bold flavors. Often made for family gatherings or special occasions, this dish is a staple in Sri Lankan cuisine, reflecting the culinary traditions of the island's diverse cultural influences.
