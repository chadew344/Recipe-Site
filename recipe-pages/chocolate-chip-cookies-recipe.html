Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies


★★★★★ 2000 Ratings

Description:

There’s nothing more comforting than a warm, chewy chocolate chip cookie fresh from the oven. These classic chocolate chip cookies are the perfect combination of crispy edges and a soft, chewy center. Made with high-quality chocolate chips, butter, and a touch of vanilla extract, they offer a perfect balance of sweetness and richness. These cookies are the ultimate treat for any occasion, whether you’re sharing them with friends, enjoying them with a glass of milk, or just indulging in a sweet moment alone. Simple, delicious, and incredibly satisfying, this recipe is a must-try for every cookie lover.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Oven and Baking Sheets

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

Step 2: Cream the Butter and Sugars

  1. In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to cream 1 cup unsalted butter with 3/4 cup granulated sugar and 3/4 cup brown sugar.
  2. Beat the mixture until light and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add in the 2 tsp vanilla extract and 2 large eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

Step 3: Mix the Dry Ingredients

  1. In a separate bowl, whisk together 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 tsp baking soda, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  2. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined.

Step 4: Add the Chocolate Chips

  1. Stir in 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  2. If desired, fold in 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans work great).

Step 5: Shape the Cookies

  1. Using a tablespoon or cookie scoop, drop rounded balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  2. If you like larger cookies, scoop out slightly bigger portions of dough.

Step 6: Bake the Cookies

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are just set.
  2. For chewier cookies, remove them from the oven when the centers are still slightly soft.

Step 7: Cool and Serve

  1. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for 2-3 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  2. Serve warm for that perfect melty chocolate experience, or store in an airtight container for up to 1 week.

Tips for Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookies:

  • Chill your cookie dough for 30 minutes before baking for thicker, chewier cookies.
  • For extra crispy cookies, bake them for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  • Use a mix of dark and milk chocolate chips for a more complex flavor.
  • For softer cookies, use a bit more brown sugar than granulated sugar.

Healthier Alternatives:

While these cookies are a delicious treat, you can modify the recipe for a lighter version by using whole wheat flour, coconut oil, or replacing some of the sugar with honey or a sugar substitute. Adding oatmeal to the dough can also give the cookies a fiber boost, making them a bit healthier.

Occasions to Enjoy Chocolate Chip Cookies:

These cookies are perfect for any occasion! Whether you’re having a family movie night, celebrating a birthday, or simply craving something sweet, classic chocolate chip cookies never disappoint. They're also an excellent treat to pack for lunchboxes, share with friends at parties, or even enjoy as a late-night snack.

Fun Fact:

Chocolate chip cookies were invented in the 1930s by Ruth Wakefield, the owner of the Toll House Inn in Massachusetts. She originally added chopped-up bits of chocolate into her cookie dough, not realizing they would melt into the dough. The rest is history, as these cookies quickly became a beloved treat across the world!
